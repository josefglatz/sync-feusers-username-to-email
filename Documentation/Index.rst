===============================
Sync fe_users username to email
===============================

:Extension key:
    sync_feusers_username_to_email

:Package name:
    josefglatz/sync-feusers-username-to-email

:Version:
    |release|

:Language:
    en

    Josef Glatz & Contributors

:Rendered:

----

This TYPO3 extension takes care of syncing the `feusers.username` to `feusers.email`
field when editing frontend users in the backend.
